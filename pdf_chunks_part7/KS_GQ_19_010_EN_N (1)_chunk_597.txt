This group covers the serv ices of general and specialist hospitals, the services of medical centres, 
maternity centres, nursing homes and convalescent homes which chiefly provide in -patient services, the 
services of military base hospitals, the services of institutions serving old people in which medical 
monitoring is an essential component and the services of rehabilitation centres providing in -patient health 
care and rehabilitative therapy where the objective is to treat the patient rather than to provide long -
term support.
