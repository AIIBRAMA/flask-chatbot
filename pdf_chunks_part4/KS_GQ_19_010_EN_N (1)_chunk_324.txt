Manual on COFOG statistics  5.3. Bulgaria  
5.3.1.  Institutional ar rangements  
The Bulgarian National Statistical Institute, National Accounts Division, is responsible for the COFOG 
statistics using data collected and provided by the Ministry of Finance. There are no other institutions 
involved in the collection, specifica tion and compilation process.  
5.3.2.  Publication of data on a national level  
The COFOG analysis  (1st level) is published by the NSI.  Table 1100 is usually completed a t t+12 
months.  
5.3.3.  Revisions policy  
The revisions policy for COFOG statistics is connected with annual national accounts revisions and 
with the EDP notifications.
