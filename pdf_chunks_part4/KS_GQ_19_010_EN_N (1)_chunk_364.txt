Manual on COFOG statistics  5.9. Greece  
5.9.1.  Institutional arrangements  
The COFOG statistics are produced by the Greek Statistical Office.  
5.9.2.  Publication of data on a national level  
The COFOG analysis is not published by the statistical office but is available on Euros tat’s website.  
5.9.3.  Revisions policy  
Data for the year t, t -1, t-2 and t -3 in the COFOG table are revised every year in December. Years 
before t -3 are considered ‘final’. Nonetheless, revisions for previous years may occur for consistency 
with ESA T able 0200.
