5.5.1.  Institutional arrangements  
The COFOG statistics are produced by Statistics Denmark.  
No other institutions participate directly in the compilation of COFOG  statistics. However, Statistics 
Denmark has regular cooperation with some of the main users of th e statistics, primarily the Ministry 
of Finance, concerning supplementary and more detailed information.  
5.5.2.  Publication of data on a national level  
The COFOG analysis  is published annually a t a 2-digit modified level by the Statistical Office.
