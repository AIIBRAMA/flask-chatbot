5.22.7.  Consumption of fixed capital expenditure  
Data on the stock and consumption of fixed capital by NACE form the b asis of the estimation of 
P.51c in the general government sector for the purposes of table 1100. The estimation is based on 
the average annual value of fixed assets at replacement prices and depreciation rates differentiated 
for kinds of fixed assets and a ctivities. The depreciation rates take into consideration the real 
economical lives of assets. Consumption of fixed capital by function for the general government 
sector is obtained through a bridge between NACE and COFOG.
