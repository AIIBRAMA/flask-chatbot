5.27.7.  Consumption of fixed capital expenditure  
Method 2, the "straight line" method is used, i.e. the value of a fixed asset is written off at a constant 
rate over the whole lifetime of the asset . This Perpetual  Inventory Method (PIM) for calculating the 
stock  of fixed assets uses long series of gross fixed capital formation (from 1975), price indices, and 
assumptions of service lives, survival/mortality functions and depreciation patterns.  
5.27.8.  Methodological soundness  
The Eurostat method is used for consolidation.  
5.27.9.  One-off transactions  
There are no large one -off transactions in the years 1998 -2017 affecting COFOG time series.
