07. HEALTH  
Comment: In division 07, no amounts for D.41 and D.8 are expected.  
Government outlays on health include expenditure  on services provided to individual persons and 
services provided on a collective basis. Expenditures on individual services are allocated to groups 
(07.1) through (07.4); expenditure  on collective services are assigned to groups (07.5) and (07.6) .

government policy; setting and enforcement of standards for medical and paramedical personnel and 
for hospitals, clinics, surgeries, etc.; regulation and licensing of providers of health services; and applied
