5.30.6.  Individual and collective final consumption 
expenditure  
The split between individual and collective final consumption expenditure is easy to calculate from 
the COFOG data which are derived from administrative accounting data.  Individual consumption 
expenditure is the main part of COFOG  expenditure classified to health, education and social 
protection, excluding R&D and other expenditure not elsewhere classified.
