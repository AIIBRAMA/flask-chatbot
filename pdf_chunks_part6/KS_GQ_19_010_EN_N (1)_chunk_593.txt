07.21  GENERAL MEDICAL SERV ICES (IS)  
This class  covers the services provided by general medical clinics and general medical practitioners. 
General medical clinics are defined as institutions which chiefly provide outpatient services which are 
not limited to a particular medical specialty and which are chiefly delivered by qualified medical doctors. 
General medical practitioners do not specialize in a particular medical specialty.
